About us




Sight and Life (SAL) is a pioneer of nutrition innovations based on science and working towards eradicating malnutrition in women of childbearing age and their children. To catalyze science-based solutions, we work with a distinguished alliance of academia, partners, and funders to advance research to inform, support, design, and incubate evidence-based malnutrition solutions at the convergence of the public and private sectors.

Our work




Sight and Life has established a distinguished alliance consisting of academia, research partners, and funders working collectively to eliminate all forms of malnutrition. Together, we discover and implement sustainable solutions, grounded on solid scientific evidence, to improve the lives of those in most need. Our partnerships are based on the principles of mutual trust, transparency, accountability, and shared goals.

Our mission




Supply

Sight and Life implements innovative approaches to increase egg production and consumption in LMICs.

Demand

Consumer preferences and dietary beliefs strongly influence demand for eggs in addition to non-availability and economic barriers.

Science & Environment

Eggs are a high-density food product that provide besides high-quality protein significant amounts of essential vitamins, minerals and fatty acidy for human nutrition.

Economics

Poultry production in LMICs provides households with an important source of protein and income.
